A Class I'm Taking: Computer Science

Why did I take Computer Science?

As I mentioned before in my major page, I went into this second year of college without really an idea of what I wanted to do.
Since taking this class, I've reignited an old passion, and possible career choice; coding.
When I was around 12 years old, I taught myself how to code in LUA, the coding language of a gaming platform called Roblox.


Figure 8 (The Roblox Logo)


Since taking this computer science class (taught by Tom Stokke), I've remembered how fun coding and working with computers really is!
I'm finally enjoying something school-related, and it's becoming a passion of mine.

So far, some of the work has been a little challenging, but nothing I can't figure out if I put my mind to it.
The classwork is (for the most part) fun, and the lectures are entertaining! It's always nice watching a lecture when
a teacher can crack a few jokes and seem relatable with the class. Overall, I'm happy I took it!
